Analysing Stock Market Dynamics using Cellular Automata

Meaning of Symbols:

  • P_D : Probability of an Active Trader (+1) Becoming Inactive (0) under the given Neighbourhood Conditions
  • P_H : Probability of an Inactive (0) Trader Becoming Active (+1 or -1) under the given Neighbourhood Conditions
  • P_C : Probability of an Inactive (0) Trader Becoming Active (+1 or -1) Irrespective of its Neighbourhood Conditions
